Discovery Guide

This guide aims to boost the discovery of your data by guiding you through a simple process
and providing you with the tools to make your data more FAIR.

(Findable, Accessible, Interopeable, Reusable)

To make data reusable first we need to make that data findable.
Not only by humans but also by computers and search engines.

Each search engine or schemas have their own required set of fields.
Fulfilling those requirements will make your data more findable.

Use our guide to optimise the findability of your dataset in a fast and easy way!
